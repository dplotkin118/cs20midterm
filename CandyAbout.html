About Us

History of Billy Bonka: The Billy Bonka Candy Shop was developed in 2011 after a groundbreaking discovery regarding 0 sugar chocalate bars. These bars were found to be extremely healthy and have been proven to contribute to a longer life. This led to the development of several other chocolate and candy bars and eventually led to the opening of our first shop in Medford MA.

Our inspiration: We found our inspiration from the famous Willy Wonka Choclate Factory. The Golden Ticket idea is something we hope to get running ASAP and in general the success and fame of the Willy Wonka factory gave us the motivation to find the tastiest and healthiest chocolate bars in the world.

What we have coming in the future: We plan to open several other shops all across the country and eventually plan to go global. In addition to this, we are currently developing healthy and affordable ice cream. Expect to see that in June of 2023.

How you can get involved: We are constantly hiring talented salesman and chefs to help expand our thriving brand. If you are interested in joining the Billy Bonka team, contact billybonka@gmail.com.
